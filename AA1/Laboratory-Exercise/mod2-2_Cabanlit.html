Banana Fish

A japanese teenager named Eiji Okumura was brought to New York by a photojournalist named Shunichi Ibe, to document street gangs, and that's where he meet Ash Lynx, the protagonist of Banana Fish. They've developed a deep bond of friendship and eventually eiji got involved in the dangers of ash's world. The series shows us the friendship and sacrifices of the main characters, their traumatic past and how they've faced the troubles brought by the people who are chasing after ash suspecting that the unknown drug is in his possession.
