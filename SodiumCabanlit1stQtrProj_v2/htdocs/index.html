About us

DEBOSTONE, INC a Filipino-owned importer company that specializes in the distribution of Korean products- STONE PLASTER.

As part of Debostone Inc.'s thrust to increase efficiency without sacrificing quality, we would like to present a new solution. Studies show that our products will reduce time spent in painting by 75-90% (in terms of man days)while at the same time minimize your total labor and material cost for coating.

Debostone Inc., has been supplying its product Stone Plaster nationwide since 2015, a water based mixes of natural stone combined with binding agents that can be applied to any surface shuch as, wood, metal and concrete for interior and exterior wall application. Since we operated are products are being used by renowned shopping malls, real estate developers, warehouses, resorts, and residential buildings all over the country.
